FAQ

Answers, before you ask.

Will the AI sound robotic and embarrass my brand?
No. We use the latest natural voice models and train on a recording of your office tone, plus the exact phrases your team uses. Most callers can't tell — and the ones who can usually say "wait, you're an AI? That's amazing." We let you listen to a live demo with your script before you ever go live.
How long does setup take?
Most clinics are live in 5–7 business days. We handle phone forwarding, calendar integration, custom scripts, voice training, and CRM connection — your team has to do almost nothing.
What if the AI can't handle a complex situation?
It hands off to a real human on your team — instantly via warm transfer, or with a beautifully written ticket if no one's available. You set the rules. Nothing falls through the cracks.
Is this HIPAA compliant?
Yes. We sign a BAA, encrypt all data in transit and at rest, and only collect the minimum PHI needed to book and confirm appointments. We follow strict access controls and audit logging.
What CRMs and calendars do you integrate with?
Boulevard, Jane, Mindbody, Vagaro, Dentrix, Open Dental, Eaglesoft, Google Calendar, Outlook, Calendly, GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Salesforce, and 30+ more. If we don't natively connect, we Zapier it in 24 hours.
What if I already have a receptionist?
Even better. Response Flow handles the calls your receptionist can't — after hours, during procedures, when they're already on a line. Most clinics see their existing front-desk close 2–3× more in-room because they're no longer drowning in the phone.
How is the guarantee actually structured?
If Response Flow doesn't generate at least 3× your monthly fee in tracked booked revenue within 60 days, we either (a) keep working for free until it does, or (b) refund 100% of what you've paid us — your choice. We can offer this because we only work with businesses we know we can win for.
